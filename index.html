STRAVA Monthly Club Reports

Usage

Firtsly, obtain this Chrome plugin, then go to your club's Recent Activity tab on Strava and hit Generate Reports in the upper right corner. A table will pop-up showing all club members' activity. You can then hit Load More to load more activities into the report. Not sure what is the limit tho. Hit Download csv and upload this csv into the field below. Chose desired month and then hit Show. The Report will then show total club kilometers acquired by bike activities and total club kilometers acquired by all other activities. The same applies for the generated leaderboard.
